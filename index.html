Polaris IT Services

We are a small company that carries out IT projects such as ATM software updates, PC installations and EPOS installations throughout the North-East of England and beyond.

If you are looking to source additional manpower to help with your IT projects, we are here to help! We have decades of experience in the IT sector and have experience carrying out work for many High Street names over the years, including UK banks, big box retailers and supermarkets.

Projects we can help with include :

EPOS installations and upgrades
Desktop PC/laptop/tablet installations/upgrades
ATM/ASC/kiosk software updates and migrations
Network equipment installations (switches/routers/WiFi access point etc.)
Peripheral installations/configuration (printers, scanners, monitors etc.)
Desktop PC/laptop software builds using SCCM etc.

We also have extensive experience in IT support, troubleshooting both hardware and software at second level.

Based in Stockton-on-Tees, areas covered include Teesside, County Durham, North Yorkshire, York, Leeds and Tyne & Wear.
